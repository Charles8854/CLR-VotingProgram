Constitution of Oregon
1996 EDITION
ARTICLE 18; Schedule
Section 7: Former Laws continued in force.
 

         All laws in force
in the Territory of Oregon when this Constitution
takes effect
and consistent therewith,
shall continue in force
until altered, or repealed.
